
ACE inhibitor lisinopril (Prinivil and Zestril) are effective at treating blood pressure.

Rizatriptan (Maxalt, Maxalt-MLT) are used to treat migraine because they block pain pathways in the brain.

Penicillin or amoxicillin is the antibiotic of choice to treat group A strep pharyngitis.

Aspirin is in a group of medications called salicylates. It works by stopping the production of certain natural substances that cause fever, pain, swelling, and blood clots.
